Fashion Conscious!! — Naaa..

April 28, 2008

Summer is here! The reason I am writing this, is to share a really funny incident.

So I get this really cool electric shaver as a gift. When I get something new, I kind of tend to experiment with it a lot — and sometimes things go wrong.

Day 1: I charge it up to the max. I have this tendency to use things right away. The first day was a total bummer — but the enthusiasm is still high. New shaver!

Day 2–4: Off to Mysore for a summer camp, teaching kids yoga in the morning and attending sessions afterwards. No time to shave. Beard: 5 days old!

Day 5: Back to Bangalore, Monday, used my regular blade for a quick shave.

Day 5 evening: Finally! 20 minutes giving myself a "new summer look." All the reading about fashion statements in Bangalore Times has finally paid off!

Day 6: The "new look" me climbs the office bus. Fellow mates are ready with their comments.

"En saar, eno change kaanastha ide" (Sir, you look different?)

"Hun saar, summer lookku — hengide?" (Yes, my new summer look — how is it?)

"Super aagi ide saar!" (Wonderful!) — I see a few grins. Einstein 1, TIME 0!

In office, my friend finally notices. His verdict? "Haha — charm kalkondbityallo macha!" (You've lost your charm dude!) Einstein 1, TIME 1.

Lunch time — a team member starts: "Datta, you are looking different today... but I really can't pinpoint the change." After some drama, the verdict: "You look quite young! And much better now!" Einstein 2, TIME 1!

So this summer, I'm sporting a new look — try and imagine me without a moustache! 😄
