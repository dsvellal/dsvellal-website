About

Back in 2007, I started my career at IBM India Software Labs, where I filed patents, won hackathons, and discovered a deep love for software craftsmanship. Since then, I've had the privilege of building software and leading teams at an insurance-tech startup, an e-commerce giant, and a global healthcare innovator.

My focus today is leading software excellence at Philips North America — helping teams ship faster, reduce technical debt, and build robust CI/CD pipelines. I've directed initiatives that cut release times by 60%, saved €2.1M through debt reduction in a single year, and launched the global 'Bar Raiser' hiring program that trained 50+ engineers.

Before Philips, I spent three years at Amazon, where I reduced fraud-check latency by 89%, built APIs for Amazon Pay's launch, and automated the daily ingestion of 2 billion+ ASINs for analytics.

When I'm not deep in code and strategy, you'll find me at the intersection of psychology and spirituality — I hold an MSc in Yoga and a certified instructor qualification from S-VYASA. I lead book distribution drives reaching 10,000+ children in rural India, conduct storytelling sessions drawn from Indian epics, and mentor graduates through community programs. I draw as much from the Mahabharata as I do from design patterns.
